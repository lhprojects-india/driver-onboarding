## Role Page

**Title:** Driver role

**Content:**
As a Laundryheap Partner Driver, you will be responsible for completing a series of essential delivery and collection tasks that ensure smooth daily operations and excellent customer experience.

**Your main responsibilities include:**

**1. Facility Pick-Up**
Your route begins at one of our Laundryheap facilities, where you will collect processed items that are ready to be delivered to customers. Ensure that all items are correctly scanned and securely loaded into your vehicle.

**2. Customer Delivery**
Deliver the collected items directly to customers' addresses. Accuracy, professionalism, and punctuality are key â€” our goal is to provide every customer with a seamless and reliable delivery experience.

**3. Customer Pick-Up**
While on your route, you will also collect unprocessed laundry items from customers. These items must be properly tagged, scanned, and handled with care before being transported back to the facility.

**4. Facility Drop-Off**
At the end of your route, return to the facility to drop off all collected (unprocessed) items. This step completes the service cycle and ensures items are ready for processing.

## How Route Works Page

**Title:** Routes and Task Addition

**Content:**
Our role structure is designed to ensure fair pay for both drivers and tasks completed rather than on hourly basis.

Each block comes with a minimum number of included tasks, meaning those tasks are already paid for as part of the block fee.

For example, a 4-hour Medium Density block includes 12 tasks, so the first 12 tasks are considered included in your block fee. If you complete more than the included tasks â€” let's say 15 tasks instead of 12 â€” the additional 3 tasks (13th, 14th, 15th) will be paid as bonus tasks, meaning you'll earn additional money for each of those extra tasks completed. This allows you to boost your earnings by taking on more tasks during your block time.

Further, our operations team may also add on-demand tasks to your route (if you have sufficient capacity and distance), allowing you to increase your earnings by completing more than the minimum included tasks that were scheduled. These added tasks are always considered bonus tasks and add to your total earnings beyond the base block fee.

Each block allocates a different guaranteed fee for low with a minimum number of tasks that need to be completed.

**Block Density Types:**
- **High-Task Density** - higher pay with more tasks requiring higher starting density.
- **Medium Density** - balanced route with good distance between tasks.
- **Low-Task Density** - Fewer number of tasks and higher driving distance.

Each block, at the fee can be viewed in the Laundryheap Driver Application.

## Cancellation Policy Page

**Title:** Block Release and Cancellation Policy

**Content:**
Understanding how to manage your scheduled blocks is essential for smooth operations and to avoid unnecessary fees. Please review the following guidelines carefully.

**1. Cancelling a Block**

If you have selected a block â€” for example, 16th January at 5:00 PM â€” you can release (cancel) that block up to 48 hours before the scheduled start time.

In this case, you must release it before 5:00 PM on 14th January.

Releasing blocks in advance allows the team to reassign the slot efficiently and ensures consistent service coverage.

**Important:** If you fail to release a block with a 48-hour notice, it will be treated as a cancellation.

**Important:** A cancellation fee will apply as per Laundryheap's predefined policy. The cancellation fee will be your entire block fee.

**2. Block Release Fee**

If you release a block with more than 48 hours notice you will be charged 10% of the block fee as a block release fee.

## Smoking Fitness Check Page

**Title:** Smoking habits policy / Physical fitness check

**Smoking habits policy:**

Please note that we strictly advise all partner drivers to handle customer orders with the utmost care. Smoking inside the vehicle is strictly prohibited, as it can cause the orders to absorb a smoke smell, which negatively affects the customer experience.

If you feel the urge to smoke, please step outside the vehicle and ensure you are far enough away so that the smoke does not enter the vehicle from the outside.

Kindly note that if we receive customer feedback regarding a smoke smell on the orders, the partner driver will be held liable for any compensation required. In addition, their access to the platform may be revoked, as such incidents severely impact customer satisfaction and brand standards.
